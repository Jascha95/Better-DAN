19th International Symposium on Implementation and Application of Functional Languages

IFL 2007

Freiburg, Germany, 27-29 September 2007

Co-located with ICFP 2007 (1-3 October 2007)

Registration

The early registration deadline for IFL 2007 has expired on 1 September 2007. For late registrations, please proceed as follows:

  1. Write an email to stating the you want to participate in IFL 2007. The email should contain your full name, your affiliation, your email address, and (possibly) your dietary restrictions.
  2. Pay the registration fee by cash once you arrive at the conference venue. The late registration fee is 85 Euro. There is no reduction for late registrations.

The registration fee includes

  • draft proceedings,
  • official LNCS proceedings,
  • drinks and light refreshments during all coffee breaks,
  • lunches on all three days,
  • public transport tickets (see below),
  • excursion and banquet dinner.

Additional social event packages (excursion and banquet dinner) for accompanying persons are available for 60 Euro. Please note the number of additional social event packages available is limited.
