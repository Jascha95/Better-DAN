IFL 2007

Freiburg, Germany, 27-29 September 2007

Co-located with ICFP 2007 (1-3 October 2007)

Conference Venue

The conference takes place at the Faculty of Applied Sciences of the University of Freiburg. The venue is marked on this map. WLAN is available at the conference venue.

You can reach the conference venue from the main train station by taking the "Breisgau S-Bahn" from "Freiburg - Hbf." to "FR Messe/Universität". Here is a timetable. Please note that most but not all trains stop at the conference venue. Also, some trains only run Monday to Friday ("Montag - Freitag") or on Saturday ("Samstag").
