The registration fee includes

  • drinks and light refreshments during all coffee breaks,
  • lunches on all three days,
  • official proceedings (except for the "Workshop on Mechanizing Metatheory" and the workshop "Commercial Users of Functional Programming").
Participation in the ICFP conference dinner incurs an extra fee of 82 USD (60 Euro) per person. For online registrations, all fees are charged in USD. For on-site registrations, all fees are charged in Euro.

Students with an accepted paper may apply for travel fellowships from the PAC fund. Moreover, PAC also provides Companion Travel Grants. These grants support travel costs for companions of SIGPLAN members with physical disabilities or small children. For details on the PAC program, see the PAC web page.

Please use the online registration page to register for ICFP 2007. The early registration deadline is September, 7th, 2007 (including).
